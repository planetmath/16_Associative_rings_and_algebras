annihilator is an ideal


The right annihilator of a right R-module MR in R is an ideal.

Proof:
By the distributive law for modules, it is easy to see that r.ann⁡(MR) is closed under addition and right multiplication. Now take x∈r.ann⁡(MR) and r∈R.

Take any m∈MR. Then m⁢r∈MR, but then (m⁢r)⁢x=0 since x∈r.ann⁡(MR). So m⁢(r⁢x)=0 and r⁢x∈r.ann⁡(MR).

An equivalent result holds for left annihilators.
