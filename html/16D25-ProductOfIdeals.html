product of ideals


Let R be a ring, and let A and B be left (right) ideals of R. Then the product of the ideals A and B, which we denote A⁢B, is the left (right) ideal generated by all products a⁢b with a∈A and b∈B. Note that since sums of products of the form a⁢b with a∈A and b∈B are contained simultaneously in both A and B, we have A⁢B⊂A∩B.
